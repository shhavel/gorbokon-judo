SUMMER CAMP 2009

In June 2009, passed our Summer Judo Camp (in Crimea, Mezhvodnoe, Sovetskaya str. 3).

Camp program:
Morning training: general physical training (running, pulling, lifting, rope, exercises for the press, push-ups from the floor). Control of quantitative indicators, a gradual increase of training load.
Evening training: studying of classical judo and techniques workout, choreography, acrobatics.
Exclusive Master-class by coach of the highest category in judo - Chesebiev B.M..

Leisure activities, visiting the sea, games, amateur concerts.
